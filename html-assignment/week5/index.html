About Me

My name is Charlene Staughton and this is my 1st website. I am a very passionate and goal-oriented individual. When I am curious about something I try to teach myself more about whatever it is. After school, I want to do more traveling and make a photo journal of my travels. I would like to build a brand for myself. I would also be interested in working for a company like National Geographic. I have always been very interested in cameras. I got my first camera when I was ten years old and have had a love for photography since. I enjoy exploring the different settings and features that my Cannon Rebel camera has. I am so excited to see what the future holds for me. There is not limiting myself on what I can and can't do in life. When I put my mind to it, there is no stopping me. As Jim Carrey once said:
Maybe other people will try to limit me but I don't limit myself. -Jim Carrey
I am very excited to be in the IMD program. I look forward to developing new skills and challenging myself. I have some revelant experience in the field. I have dabbled in Adobe programs like Photoshop and have created magazines and posters. I've done some work in videography and produced a few movie trailers with friends. I am currently learning how to perfect my skills in Adobe Illustrator which has been both challenging and a lot of fun. So far, I have recieved positive feedback on the work I have done. This makes me proud of myself and motivated to create more. I enjoy starting new projects and thinking outside the box! I love to draw out my ideas with pencil and paper. This helps me collect all of my thoughts in one place and start my creative process.
